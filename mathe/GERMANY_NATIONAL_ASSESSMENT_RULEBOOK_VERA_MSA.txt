GERMANY – NATIONAL ASSESSMENT ALIGNED TEST GENERATION RULEBOOK
Aligned with KMK Educational Standards (Bildungsstandards)
Covers: VERA 3, VERA 8, Zentrale Klassenarbeit, MSA (Sek I)
Purpose: AI-driven realistic German standardized test generation

1. OVERVIEW OF GERMAN ASSESSMENT STRUCTURE
Primary Assessment:
- VERA 3 (Grade 3 comparative assessment)

Lower Secondary Assessment:
- VERA 8 (Grade 8 comparative assessment)
- Zentrale Klassenarbeiten (state-wide written exams)
- MSA – Mittlerer Schulabschluss (Grade 10 final level, structure relevant from Grade 8)

Assessments focus on competencies, not only procedural calculation.
2. COMPETENCY DOMAINS (KMK STRUCTURE)
Inhaltsbezogene Kompetenzen:
- Zahlen und Operationen
- Größen und Messen
- Raum und Form
- Funktionaler Zusammenhang
- Daten und Zufall

Prozessbezogene Kompetenzen:
- Problemlösen
- Argumentieren
- Modellieren
- Darstellen
- Kommunizieren
3. VERA 3 STRUCTURE (GRADE 3)
Duration: 45–60 minutes
Focus Areas:
- Multiplication tables
- Written addition/subtraction
- Basic division
- Simple geometry
- Data interpretation

Question Types:
- Short answer
- Multiple choice
- Structured reasoning
- Word problems

Difficulty Distribution:
40% basic
40% medium
20% advanced reasoning
4. VERA 8 STRUCTURE (GRADE 8)
Duration: 90 minutes
Focus Areas:
- Percent calculations
- Linear equations
- Proportional reasoning
- Geometry (area, volume)
- Data analysis

Question Types:
- Multi-step word problems
- Justification tasks (begründe)
- Modeling tasks
- Diagram interpretation

Anforderungsbereiche:
I – Reproduktion
II – Zusammenhänge herstellen
III – Verallgemeinern und Reflektieren
5. ZENTRALE KLASSENARBEIT STRUCTURE
Typical Sections:
Teil A – Grundaufgaben (Reproduktion)
Teil B – Anwendung
Teil C – Komplexe Problemlösung

Points: 30–50
Partial scoring mandatory.
Real-life context tasks included.
6. MSA-STYLE STRUCTURE (Sekundarstufe I Level)
Duration: 120 minutes
Sections:
- Non-calculator part
- Calculator part
- Modeling tasks
- Extended reasoning tasks

Content Includes:
- Linear functions
- Systems of equations
- Geometry (Pythagoras)
- Percent and interest
- Probability

Operatoren Examples:
berechne
bestimme
begründe
erläutere
interpretiere
7. DIFFICULTY DISTRIBUTION MODEL
30% Anforderungsbereich I
45% Anforderungsbereich II
25% Anforderungsbereich III

Tasks increase in complexity toward the end of the test.
8. SCORING MODEL
Raw Score → Grade (Germany 1–6 scale):
90–100% → Note 1
80–89% → Note 2
65–79% → Note 3
50–64% → Note 4
30–49% → Note 5
0–29% → Note 6

Rubric-based scoring required for reasoning tasks.
Partial credit allowed.
9. AI TEST GENERATION RULES
- Only generate tasks from curriculum progression already covered.
- Include both inhaltsbezogene and prozessbezogene competencies.
- Include real-life modeling tasks.
- Ensure presence of justification questions (Begründung erforderlich).
- Separate calculator / non-calculator sections for Sek I level.
- Provide marking scheme and expected solution path.
